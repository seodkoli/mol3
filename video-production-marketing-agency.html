Frequently Asked Questions

What falls under video production?

Video production encompasses a wide range of tasks and activities, including conceptualizing video ideas, scripting, storyboarding, filming, editing, adding visual effects, sound design, and distribution of the finished product.

Are video editors still in demand?

Yes, video editors are still in high demand, especially with the increasing popularity of video content across various platforms such as social media, streaming services, and online marketing. Businesses, content creators, and organizations continue to seek skilled video editors who can produce high-quality videos that capture and retain audience attention.

What's the difference between filmmaking and production?

Filmmaking is the process of creating films, which involves storytelling through the medium of moving images. Production, on the other hand, refers to the overall management and coordination of the various elements involved in creating a video or film, including planning, filming, and post-production.
